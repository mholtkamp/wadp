GDSync Launches

Today marks the start of GDsync, a game development network for indie projects. Creating and managing projects has never been easier. Are you a developer looking for an artist? Use the search feature to find artists and invite them to the project!

GDSync features:

  • Create a profile to showcase your talent!
  • Create and manage projects
  • Message other users
  • Subscribe to projects to stay up to date
  • Sell and market your finished games
